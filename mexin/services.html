core services

New Build Inspection

When building a new home it is extremely important to have your home examined for construction-related problems. Typically 20-30 problems with design, workmanship, and design are found and most are unknown to the home owner until the inspection is completed. Ottawa Home Inspectors helps find these problems so they can be fixed before they cause problems in the future.

Resale Inspection

Before buying a new home it is important to know what you are getting yourself into. Our inspections reveal any issues that the house you are looking at. However, it is important to remember that the inspection cannot reveal everything that could go wrong with the house in the future. For example, finding the current plumbing leak does not mean that there won't be any plumbing issues in the future.
